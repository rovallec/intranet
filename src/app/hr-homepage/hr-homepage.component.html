Survey Results

We are running our periodic Employee Satisfaction Survey and we would like to invite everyone to participate. Part of the results from the past surveys helped us improve some of our processes to enhance employee experience. Some of the changes we implemented after hearing employees reviews were: Payroll Query response time Earlier and more timely release of payslips More timely communication of performance standing Annual Performance Reviews Some of these are still work in progress but we have initiated plans to see it through and implement long term. Your opinions matter and we would love to hear from each one of you. We will close the survey on June 15, 2021 so please take a few minutes to respond so that your voice may be heard. Thank you and we look forward to your participation.
